"ESCALATE") FIRST, then the specific recommendations as a small
follow-on. This is the verb-after-posture register that reads as
"what the system is doing" first, "what action you can take" second.

has Intent REFUSED. For non-refused cases, keep the (iii-a) verb-
first treatment. The detection: scan `reads` for any specialist
